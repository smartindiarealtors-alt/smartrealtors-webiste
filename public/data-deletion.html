Data Deletion Instructions

Learn how you can request deletion of your personal information from Smart India Realtors.

1. Introduction

At Smart India Realtors, we value the trust you place in us and respect your control over your personal data. In alignment with global privacy regulations and our commitment to transparency, users have the right to request deletion of their personal information that we have collected, stored, or processed.

2. Information Eligible for Deletion

You may request the deletion of data that you have shared with us during your interactions with our website, listing tools, or marketing campaigns. Eligible data categories include, but are not limited to:

  • • Name: Your first name, last name, or aliases recorded in our databases.
  • • Phone Number: Registered contact number used for scheduling property updates.
  • • Email Address: Email identification used to receive brochures and project pricing.
  • • Property Preferences: Recorded preferences regarding budgets, configurations, and property locations.
  • • Contact Form Information: Message details, queries, and descriptions submitted through website forms.
  • • Lead Form Information: Contact detail combinations captured through Facebook/Instagram Meta Lead Ads or Google Ads.

3. How to Request Deletion

To initiate a data deletion request, you are required to submit an email to our support team. Please send an email to:

Email: smartindiarealtors@gmail.com

To ensure we locate and delete the correct records, please include the following details in your email:

  • • Full Name: The name associated with your property search profile.
  • • Registered Phone Number: The mobile contact number used in our forms.
  • • Registered Email Address: The email address linked to your account or inquiries.
  • • Reason for Request (optional): Feedback explaining why you would like your data deleted.

4. Verification Process

To safeguard user data against unauthorized access or malicious deletion requests, we must verify your identity before processing any deletion. Identity verification may be required before deleting personal information. This process may involve sending a verification code (OTP) to your registered phone number, requesting confirmation from your registered email address, or checking matching validation credentials.

5. Processing Time

Once your identity has been successfully validated, we will immediately initiate the database removal process. Data deletion requests are generally processed within 7–15 business days. You will receive an email confirmation once the removal of your data from our active systems is complete.

6. Exceptions

Please note that some information may be retained if legally required. Certain transaction details, billing items, or communications may be held in archival databases to satisfy statutory financial audits, legal dispute records, regulatory compliance mandates, or fraud prevention requirements. Once these statutory retention timelines expire, the data will be securely purged.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions or require support regarding your data deletion rights, please reach out to us:

Smart India Realtors

Email: smartindiarealtors@gmail.com

Website: https://www.smartindiarealtors.in
