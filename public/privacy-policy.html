Privacy Policy

Smart India Realtors values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Introduction

Welcome to Smart India Realtors. We value your trust and are highly committed to protecting the privacy of your personal data. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, store, and process your information when you visit our website (https://www.smartindiarealtors.in) or interact with our services.

By accessing our website and using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms outlined here, please do not use our site or submit any information through our forms.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you make inquiries about properties, register on our website, or interact with our online forms and advertising campaigns. This information includes, but is not limited to:

  • • Name: To address you personally and manage our customer relationships.
  • • Phone Number: To contact you regarding property inquiries, site visits, and updates.
  • • Email Address: To send you property details, brochures, and support assistance.
  • • Property Preferences: Budget, location preferences, and desired configurations to curate relevant listings.
  • • Form Submissions: Any messages, comments, or additional details submitted through our contact and query forms.

How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for purposes based on legitimate business interests, the fulfillment of our contract with you, compliance with our legal obligations, and/or your consent. We use the information we collect to:

  • • Contact Regarding Property Enquiries: Connect with you to answer questions about projects you showed interest in.
  • • Schedule Site Visits: Coordinate convenient times for you to inspect residential or commercial properties in person.
  • • Share Project Details: Send marketing material, brochures, price sheets, and construction updates.
  • • Customer Support: Respond to your feedback, resolve queries, and assist with documentation.
  • • Improve Services: Analyze web traffic patterns and user behavior to refine our platform layout, listings, and customer outreach.

Data Protection

We implement a variety of standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. This includes secure data storage facilities, encrypted transmission channels, and access restrictions to ensure only authorized personnel handle your information. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third Party Services

We use third-party analytics and marketing services to measure website traffic and display relevant advertisements. Specifically:

  • • Google Analytics: Helps us collect insights about website usage, audience demographics, and page interactions to enhance user experience.
  • • Meta Lead Ads: We run advertising campaigns on Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram). Leads captured via Meta Lead Ads are transferred securely to our systems to enable prompt follow-up.

Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be recalled by a web server. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, track users' movements, and gather demographic information. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level, but choosing to disable cookies may limit your use of certain features or functions on our website.

Data Sharing

Your personal information is never sold, traded, or rented to third parties for marketing purposes. We only share your data with trusted partners and service providers when necessary to provide the services you requested (e.g. sharing your preferences with financial institutions for home loans, or developers of projects you inquired about). All such partners are required to maintain strict confidentiality and protect your data.

User Rights

Depending on your location and local laws, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data. These rights include:

  • • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • • Right to Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • • Right to Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data when it is no longer required for the purpose it was collected.
